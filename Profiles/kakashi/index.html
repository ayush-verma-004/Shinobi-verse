Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake is a calm, highly skilled ninja known for his mastery of the Sharingan and his signature mask. He's a brilliant strategist and a respected mentor with a mysterious past.

Kakashi Sharingan

Kakashi received his Sharingan from Obito Uchiha during a mission as a gift. Though not an Uchiha, he could use it with great skill, mastering powerful techniques like Kamui. The Sharingan enhanced his perception and made Chidori usable. He lost it after the war when Obito's chakra faded.

Kakashi Chidori

Kakashi's Chidori, also called Lightning Blade (Raikiri), is a powerful lightning-style jutsu he created. It channels intense lightning chakra into his hand, producing a high-speed thrust attack. He could only master it after gaining the Sharingan, which helped him counter its speed. It's famous for its piercing power and signature chirping sound
